Koh Tao Resort Location

Discover Monkey Flower Villas

Monkey Flower Villas is a premier Koh Tao family villa resort, perfectly situated in the lush hills above Sairee Beach. Recognized among the best ocean view villas Koh Tao has to offer, our property provides breathtaking panoramic views of the Gulf of Thailand while still being just minutes away from the heart of the island’s most vibrant village.

Nestled in the tranquil hills of the northwestern coast, our Koh Tao villas with sunset view provide the ideal balance of privacy and convenience. Finding a private villa rental near Sairee Beach means you are never far from the action, whether you’re looking to relax in nature, explore local beaches, or enjoy the island’s nightlife.

View Location on Map

Private, Secluded Setting

Despite our proximity to town, Monkey Flower Villas feels like a world away. Surrounded by lush jungle and perched on a hillside, our location is peaceful, private, and ideal for those who want to escape the crowds while staying close to authentic island life.

Complimentary Private Shuttle Service

To make your stay as seamless as possible, we offer free round-trip transfers from the pier and complimentary shuttle service to anywhere on the island during your stay—available daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. No need to rent a scooter unless you want to!

Points of Interest Nearby

  • Sairee Village: 3 minutes
  • Mae Haad Pier: 10 minutes
  • Hin Wong Bay (Snorkeling): 10 minutes
  • Ao Leuk Beach: 15 minutes
  • Chalok Baan Kao: 15 minutes
