Orchid Villa Accommodation, Koh Tao

The “Orchid” Villa is our premier luxury residence, offering a romantic and fun escape in the heart of Koh Tao. This stunning villa features a huge master bedroom, two spacious living rooms, and unique circular bathrooms designed to flow with nature. The highlight is the award-winning private infinity-edge swimming pool & deck, offering breathtaking ocean views.

Adventure seekers can cross the bridge to the roof and jump directly into the pool! The Orchid Villa accommodates up to ten guests, all surrounded by beautiful tropical greenery.

Gardenia Villa Accommodation, Koh Tao

The Gardenia Villa is a masterpiece of eco-luxury design, built directly into the natural granite boulders of Koh Tao. This expansive three-story home features five large air-conditioned bedrooms, three bathrooms, a full kitchen, and living/dining areas. Every floor offers stunning sea views surrounded by lush foliage.

Luxury amenities include a large private swimming pool with color-changing lights, a hanging chair swing over the water, a sun deck with loungers, and a private yard. The Gardenia Villa accommodates a maximum of ten guests, sharing three king-size beds, two full-size beds, and a sleeper couch. Experience the ultimate nature retreat with your family and friends.

Bougainvillea Villa Accommodation, Koh Tao

The “Bougainvillea” villa offers an intimate luxury experience enveloped by nature. It features two air-conditioned bedrooms plus an extra air-conditioned loft. Relax in your private award-winning infinity-edge pool, complete with a natural rock waterfall and ambient lighting at night. The Bougainvillea villa accommodates up to six guests across the two bedrooms and the loft area.

*Note: The loft area is accessible via a built-in ladder and is not suitable for small children or guests with mobility issues.
