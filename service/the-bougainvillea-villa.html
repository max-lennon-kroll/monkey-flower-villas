Bougainvillea Villa Overview & Features

The Bougainvillea Villa is a perfect example of our boutique villas Koh Tao offerings. This intimate retreat has two main, air-con bedrooms, each with a full-size bed, 42″ flat-screen TV with Roku Ultra & the best and most reliable high-speed internet Wi-Fi available on Koh Tao. There is also a third large air-conditioned loft room, accessible by a ladder, that has an extra mattress for additional guests to sleep in. You will enjoy the award-winning private plunge pool. Being one of the most romantic Koh Tao villas with sunset view, it comes complete with a waterfall and colored lights at night. Not to mention our famous free 24-hour taxi service to anywhere you want to go, making it a perfect private diving retreat Koh Tao basecamp.

The “Bougainvillea” villa can accommodate a maximum of six guests, as three couples, sharing the two full-size beds in the two main rooms, as well as an extra double futon mattress made up in the loft. The loft area is accessible via a built-in ladder and is not suitable for people with mobility problems or small children.

There is a small kitchen with a fridge, cooktop, and utensils, like a blender, pots and pans, cups, plates, cutlery, etc.
