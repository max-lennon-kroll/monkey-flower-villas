Gardenia Villa Overview & Features

The Gardenia Villa represents the ultimate Koh Tao family villa resort experience, featuring five large air-conditioned bedrooms, three bathrooms, a living/dining room, and a kitchen, in a huge and beautiful three-story home, with stunning sea views from every floor.

As a premier private pool villa Koh Tao destination, amenities include sweeping views, a magnificent swimming pool, with color-changing lights at night, a chair swing hanging over the water, a sun deck with loungers, fast internet, a premium sound system, and TVs with Roku Ultra. There is a fresh coffee station provided with the best whole beans, a grinder, and a drip coffee maker. The kitchen is well appointed with necessary items like a blender, toaster, cutlery, etc… All of our villas enjoy daily or on-demand cleaning service, basic toiletries, and bath and beach towels. There is even a lovely private yard with gardens of flowers and coconut palms, establishing a true luxury wellness stay Koh Tao.

Ranked among the best ocean view villas Koh Tao has to offer, this stunning property is the perfect place to enjoy paradise with your family and friends. Though the “Gardenia” villa can accommodate a maximum of twelve guests, as six couples, sharing three king-size beds, two full-size (double) beds, and one full-size fold-out sleeper couch-bed. Group size is recommended at eight or less. Groups larger than eight may not be able to use our famous 24-hour car and driver service all at the same time.
