Orchid Villa Overview & Features

The Orchid Villa features three large air-conditioned bedrooms, equipped with three king-size beds. There are also two additional fold-out couch beds available for larger groups. As one of the finest Koh Tao luxury villa rentals, it comfortably accommodates up to six guests as three couples, but can fit up to ten guests using the full-size sleeper couch-beds, making it ideal for family holidays or group getaways.

Every detail of your secluded cliffside villa Koh Tao experience is taken care of with exceptional daily or on-demand cleaning service, including basic toiletries, as well as fresh bath and beach towels to ensure a relaxing, hotel-quality stay.

Located just a 3-minute walk (or a free 24/7 courtesy drive) down the hill to Sairee Beach—the longest and most popular beach on Koh Tao—you will have quick access to world-class scuba diving, stunning sunsets, and top local restaurants, giving you the best private villa rental near Sairee Beach.

Outside your tropical holiday home, you will find a serene pond filled with colorful fish. We also designed the lower level to be a remote work friendly luxury villa Koh Tao environment, perfect for staying connected while in paradise., a small natural waterfall just outside the master bedroom window, and fish food provided for hand-feeding them.
