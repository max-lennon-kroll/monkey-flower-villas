How do we get to Koh Tao?

There are several ways to get to Koh Tao. The fastest and easiest way is to fly directly to Koh Samui, which has hourly flights daily. From Koh Samui, both the Lomprayah and Seatran ferries offer multiple departures each day to Koh Tao. Another popular option from Bangkok is taking a sleeper train to Chumphon (first-class or second-class lower berths are highly recommended), followed by a short ferry ride. Alternatively, you can book a joint bus/ferry ticket from Bangkok via Lomprayah. If you need assistance, local travel agents like Island Travel Koh Tao or Fame Tour can easily arrange all your transportation. As one of the top Koh Tao luxury villa rentals, Monkey Flower Villas is happy to provide additional guidance!

How do we pay?

When you make your reservation through our official website for your private pool villa Koh Tao stay, payment is processed securely through PayPal, requiring a 50% holding deposit to secure your dates. If you prefer a bank transfer, we can provide the necessary wire information. The remaining 50% balance is due upon check-in. Don't worry about cash—there are plenty of banks, money exchanges, and ATMs located conveniently around Koh Tao.

What is the best way to get around Koh Tao without renting a scooter?

Navigating the island's steep hills can be intimidating, and many travelers prefer to avoid renting a scooter. While local taxis can get expensive, guests staying at Monkey Flower Villas enjoy an exclusive, complimentary 24-hour car and driver service. Whether you want a quick 3-minute ride down to Sairee Village, a drop-off at Mae Haad Pier, or a late-night lift back to your secluded cliffside villa Koh Tao, our private driver is on call to take you anywhere completely free of charge.

How can I arrange a scuba diving or snorkeling trip on Koh Tao?

Koh Tao is world-renowned for its vibrant coral reefs and marine life. You don’t need to spend hours researching dive schools or standing in lines at the pier. When you book your stay with us, our team handles all the logistics. We partner with the top-rated PADI dive centers on the island for open water courses, and we can easily arrange private longtail boat charters for secluded snorkeling excursions to spots like Shark Island or Hin Wong Bay.

Which are the best beaches to visit on Koh Tao, and how do we get to them?

The island is full of incredible coastlines, from the bustling shores of Sairee Beach (just a short drive from our hillside retreat) to the peaceful waters of Ao Leuk Beach and Shark Bay. Because some of the island's most beautiful hidden bays are down rugged roads, our complimentary island shuttle service is the perfect way to beach-hop safely. Just let your private driver know where you'd like to explore, and we'll take you there and pick you up when you're done sunbathing.

Can we see the famous Koh Tao sunset from the villas?

Absolutely. Monkey Flower Villas is perched on the scenic hillside directly above Sairee Beach, which faces due west. Every single villa features private balconies or wrap-around terraces built into the natural granite boulders, offering unobstructed, panoramic ocean views of the Gulf of Thailand—making it the ultimate private sunset viewpoint on the island.

Is breakfast included with my stay?

While breakfast is not included in the standard rate, we offer excellent breakfast delivery straight to your villa door. A full English set breakfast with eggs, bacon or sausages, potatoes, fresh baked breads, fresh coffee, and juice is available for under 200 Thai Baht per person. As a luxury wellness stay Koh Tao provider, it is also our pleasure to take you out in the morning—or any other time—to great local eateries using our free shuttle service.

Is your 24 hour car & driver service really free?

Yes! Our famous 24/7 pick-up and drop-off service is a complimentary courtesy for our guests, and a pleasure for us to provide. It is one of the many amenities that makes us the best ocean view villas Koh Tao has to offer. Tips to your driver are never expected, but always appreciated.

What is check-in & check-out time?

Normally, check-in is around 2:00 PM, and it depends on the check-out time of the previous guests. We always try to accommodate all of our guests, and will try to get you in as early as we can, and checked-out as late as possible. If your villa is not ready when you arrive on the island, our driver can safely store your bags and drop you off at a nice place for breakfast or a walk on the beach. Whenever you need a ride, simply call our onsite manager Mo or driver Steve on the complimentary mobile phone provided!

What is your cancellation policy?

Because our boutique Koh Tao family villa resort is unique and typically booked many months in advance, we can offer a full refund (excluding transaction costs) if requested no less than 45 days before your scheduled arrival. After the 45-day mark, we can still offer a refund if your reserved dates are re-booked by someone else at the regular rates.

How close is the beach & town?

It is about a ten-minute walk down the hill to the soft sands of Sairee Beach, the longest and most popular beach on Koh Tao, and another ten minutes to the center of town for shopping, dining, and nightlife. However, most of our customers enjoy using our complimentary car and driver service to explore different beaches and attractions. If you choose to walk into town but prefer a ride back, simply call your driver, and he will be right there!
