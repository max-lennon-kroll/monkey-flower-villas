Monkey Flower Villas Reviews

These are some of our great reviews, giving us a 5-star average on hundreds of Google and Tripadvisor!

All the texts are pulled directly from the originals.

"Island Luxury!"

Jane_C88 on Tripadvisor | May 2021

A group of 11 of us stayed in the large villa for a week. It was absolutely fabulous! The property itself is stunning, with views to match. You can trust the incredible photos and drone shots from their page; it’s as impressive in real life! It was spacious, clean, and comfortable, with cozy beds and hot showers.

Our trip was absolutely made by Steve, who is supposedly the “driver” but actually the man behind it all! Not only did he drive us to and from various destinations (a huge relief for the few of our party who did not want to drive motorbikes, & for preventing the whole group from having to drive at night) but he was responsive, helpful, efficient and friendly in response to inquiries and requests.

The cleaning team also deserve a shout out for keeping the villa spotless and managing to keep up with the supply of towels & linens for such a big group. This is absolutely the place for a luxury island getaway and we were not ready to leave when the week was up.

Highly recommended 🙂

"A truly sensational experience with lovely people. A must in Koh Tao if you have the opportunity."

Liz C on Tripadvisor | June 2021

We were lucky enough to stay at Monkey Flower Villas during the first Covid-19 lockdown, which meant we stayed March until July 2020. Lincoln the owner, Mo the manager and Steve made us feel so welcome and made it possible for us to extend our stay until it was safe to leave the island. For which we will always be grateful.

We stayed in Villa no 1 with our 3 year old daughter. It’s an experience we will never forget. The villa itself was so comfortable and luxurious and the view of the whole bay was sensational! Mo, Steve and the team were so hospitable and welcoming that we were very sad when we eventually came to say goodbye. We feel so fortunate that we were able to stay in such a beautiful and comfortable place and with such kind and experienced Koh Tao islanders to support us in what could have been a difficult time.

The view from the infinity pool has to be seen to be believed, the quirky details in the apartment from the floating super king bed, to the rocks incorporated the walls of the kitchen and bathroom make it a truly unique and special place to stay. The surrounding gardens are so interesting and beautifully maintained to make the most of the tropical flora and fauna.

We have memories to last a lifetime from our time in Koh Tao at Monkey Flower, including our little girl learning to swim in that amazing pool! Thank you so much Lincoln, Mo, Steve and all the wonderful team. We hope to see you all again.

Best wishes Liz, Steve & Gaia Hart

"INCREDIBLE place to stay with mind blowing views and service"

235kimberleys on Tripadvisor | April 2021

INCREDIBLE place to stay. The views (even from the bed!) are probably the best views I’ve ever had. The infinity pool looking over the beautiful Sairee beach is stunning, big and so much fun as you can jump into it from the roof (safely of course!). Such a benefit being up in the beautiful jungle surrounded by nature and lovely well maintained garden, but can walk (20min) or drive to the beach (5min).

Lying on the roof top under the stars looking over the bay was one of my favourite daily moments ❤️. The villa is very funky and unique, kitchen is a bit minimal but I don’t doubt the team will provide any bits you need. Aircon in the bedroom is great and quiet. Few bugs and frogs that can make some noise, but we LOVED the feel of being in the jungle surrounded by nature.

Muhummad, Steve and the team were so caring and efficient, making us feel unbelievably welcome, safe and looked after …. they kept cleaning the villa for us and Steve drove us anywhere is the island we wanted to go (Steve is the most efficient, attentive committed and lovely soul making our stay so wonderful).

We will be back without question if we stay in Koh Tao! Very grateful, this villa and experience made our trip ❤️
